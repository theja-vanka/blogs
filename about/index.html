About

I work at the intersection of research and production — training models, building ML systems, and closing the gap between experiment and deployment. My focus is applied computer vision: getting research ideas to actually ship in production environments.

This blog is where I write down what I learn — practical guides on model architectures, training at scale, deployment pipelines, and the engineering decisions that make research usable. I try to write the articles I wish had existed when I was figuring something out.
